About the Game

"Falling Fruits", a catch & survive game. The player controls a small brown basket at the bottom of the screen, using the arrow key on a keyboard the player can collect the falling fruits. As the game progresses, the player's score increases for each obstacle caught. Losing a fruit = live lost. All 3 lives lost triggers a Game Over screen, and the player can restart instantly.

Genre: Arcade Game
